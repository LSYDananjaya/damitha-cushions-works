## Design Context

### Users

Local customers, homeowners, vehicle owners, and small businesses evaluating Damitha Cushion Works before making a custom cushion or upholstery enquiry. They need to quickly see craft quality, service range, and a direct path to WhatsApp, phone, or email.

### Brand Personality

Hands-on, precise, and proudly local. The interface should feel like a serious workshop with visible craft, not a generic furniture catalogue.

### Aesthetic Direction

Editorial workshop portfolio with bold compressed typography, warm textile neutrals, dark leather moments, and selective color pulled from the Damitha logo and automotive orange accents. The site should feel tactile, confident, and commission-ready.

### Design Principles

- Lead with the work: product imagery, materials, and detail shots should carry the page.
- Use color like thread: small, intentional accents for service categories, CTAs, focus, and wayfinding.
- Keep layouts broad and editorial, with restrained surfaces instead of card-heavy decoration.
- Preserve motion as a craft reveal, while respecting reduced-motion users.
- Make contact paths obvious and usable on mobile and desktop.
